About

  • Version 2.0 reworked for latest iOS. Sorry for the delay. A few new tweaks for the UI.
  • Version 1.6 adds metric cut lists and larger thicknesses.
  • Version 1.5 expands the thickness options for hardwoods TO 48/4 and the quantities for lumber and timber from 50 to 100.
  • Version 1.4 introduces board feet and cost grand totals at the bottom of the board feet page.
  • Version 1.3 introduces board feet measurements for hardwoods.
  • This application was developed to help saw mill users determine the cuts to make to get the desired board thickness automatically accounting for the kerf. A board feet calculator was also added with both softwood and hardwood thickness measurements. Happy sawing.
  • Users requested log volume calculations and lumber list creation which are available in this Pro version.
  • The log volume calculations for Doyle, Scribner, and International are approximations taken from the equations at https://www.extension.purdue.edu/extmedia/FNR/FNR-191.pdf. The ROY scale is directly from the formula.
